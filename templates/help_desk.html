    Frequently Asked Questions
    What is the Metro Manila Resto Finder?
    The Metro Manila Resto Finder is a web application that helps users discover restaurants in Metro Manila, Philippines, based on their preferences and recommendations generated using a content-based recommendation system.
    How does the recommendation system work?
    Our recommendation system utilizes the cosine similarity and TF-IDF (Term Frequency-Inverse Document Frequency) algorithm to analyze restaurant data and user preferences. It suggests restaurants that are similar to the ones you have liked or visited before.
    Is the Metro Manila Resto Finder free to use?
    Yes, the Metro Manila Resto Finder is completely free to use for all users.
    Do I need to create an account to use the Resto Finder?
    No, you can use the Resto Finder without creating an account. However, creating an account allows you to save your preferences and access personalized recommendations.
    How do I search for restaurants in Metro Manila?
    You can search for restaurants by entering keywords, such as cuisine type, location, or restaurant name, in the search bar on the homepage. The system will then provide you with a list of relevant restaurants.
    Can I filter search results by cuisine type or location?
    Yes, you can use filters to narrow down your search results by cuisine type, location, price range, and other preferences.
    How accurate are the restaurant recommendations?
    The accuracy of recommendations depends on the data available and your interaction with the system. The more you use the system and provide feedback, the more accurate the recommendations become.
    Can I leave reviews and ratings for restaurants?
    Yes, you can leave reviews and ratings for restaurants you have visited. Your feedback helps other users and improves the recommendation system's accuracy.
    Is my personal information safe on the Metro Manila Resto Finder website?
    We take data privacy seriously. Your personal information is protected and not shared with third parties without your consent.
    How can I contact customer support if I have issues or questions?
    If you have any questions or encounter issues while using the Metro Manila Resto Finder, you can contact our customer support team through the "help desk" section of the website, and click the Issue Reporting System. and we will assist you promptly.
