    About us
    Welcome to our restaurant recommendation system, a web application developed by a group of passionate students from Ama Computer College. Our goal is to provide you with an enhanced dining experience by leveraging the power of advanced algorithms and cutting-edge technology.

    At Ama Computer College, we believe that innovation is key to transforming everyday activities into extraordinary experiences. With this philosophy in mind, we set out to create a restaurant recommendation system that would revolutionize how people discover new dining establishments.

    Our web application utilizes a powerful algorithm based on Term Frequency-Inverse Document Frequency (TF-IDF) and cosine similarity. This algorithm analyzes various factors, such as menu items, customer reviews, location, and more, to identify restaurants that best match your preferences. By considering both content and similarity, we ensure that the recommendations are highly relevant to your tastes.

    As students, we have dedicated countless hours to researching, designing, and developing this web application to ensure its accuracy, efficiency, and user-friendliness.

    Thank you for choosing our restaurant recommendation system. We are excited to be a part of your culinary journey, introducing you to new and delightful dining experiences. If you have any feedback or inquiries, please don't hesitate to reach out to us. Happy exploring!
